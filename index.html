Staple Foods

At the heart of many Ugandan meals are starches like posho (a thick maize porridge), matooke (steamed green bananas), cassava, sweet potatoes, and rice. Matooke, especially, is a national favorite—steamed and mashed, often served with a hearty sauce.

Sauces and Proteins

These staples are usually paired with flavorful sauces made from beans, groundnuts (peanuts), or meats.
